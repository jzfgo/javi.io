Piensa Diferente was an early startup that offered consulting services to small to medium-sized resorts and B&Bs. It specialised in offering tailored yet affordable solutions to enable its clients to compete directly with the big hospitality chains through technology and innovation.

## Responsibilities

### Development

Since the creation of the company, served as the lead developer of all hospitality solutions, including Bahiazul and Las Colinas Resort systems.

### UX / UI Design

Responsible for the UX/UI design of the Bahiazul website and Property Management System (PMS), UX design for the UMA Backoffice, and branding/logo design (including Piensa Diferente and Bahiazul logos).

### Management

Oversaw all technical aspects of the business including Cloud Architecture, IT infrastructure, and supervised external developers hired to build custom components.
