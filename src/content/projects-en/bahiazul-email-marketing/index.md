During my time at **Bahiazul Resort**, I led a series of targeted **email marketing campaigns** aimed at bringing guests back to the resort following the disruptions caused by the COVID-19 pandemic.

As **Technology Consultant**, I was responsible for the technical strategy, setup, and optimization of the campaigns.

## Approach

- **A/B Testing** to identify the most effective messaging and subject lines.
- **Segmentation** and **Dynamic Content** to tailor messages to different guest profiles.
- **Automation** workflows to send timely, personalized follow-ups.
- **Analytics** to track campaign performance and iterate on results.

## Tools

- **Campaign Monitor** for email delivery and campaign management.
