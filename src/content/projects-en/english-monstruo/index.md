**English Monstruo** is an iPhone application built for **Cambridge University Press**.

It is a "Brain Trainer" style game designed to teach English learners how to avoid the most common mistakes made in the First Certificate Exam (FCE).

## Design & Experience

As the **Game Designer** and **UX Designer**, I was in charge of:

- **Information Architecture (IA)** and flow design.
- **UX Design**.
- **Game design mechanics**, balancing educational content with a fun gameplay loop.

## Results

- Winner of the **Smile Award 2013** for its ability to blend humor and game mechanics to enhance the learning experience.
- Achieved a **5-star rating** in Apple's App Store.
