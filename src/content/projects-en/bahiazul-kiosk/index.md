**Bahiazul Digital Kiosk** is a mobile-first web application developed for **Bahiazul Resort** to replace physical printed materials with a digital alternative.

Guests scan QR codes placed around the resort to access brochures, maps, activity information, and other resort materials directly on their devices.

As **UX/UI Designer** and **Frontend Developer**, I was responsible for both the design and development of the application.

## Technologies

- **Svelte** for building the single-page application — chosen for its speed and lightweight footprint.
- **Firebase** for hosting and backend services.
- **Sketch** for UI design.
