**Telefónica SDiE 2017** was an interactive web application developed for **Fundación Telefónica** to present the data and findings of their annual report on digital society in Spain.

As **Frontend Developer** at **Piensa Diferente**, I built an interactive single-page application that brought the report's data to life through charts and visualizations.

## Technologies

- **React** for the single-page application.
- **Recharts** for data visualization and interactive charts.
