Since the state of alarm was declared in Spain and other countries, many companies — large and small — have been opening up access to their paid services for free, with the aim of helping other businesses, freelancers, and citizens get through this crisis.

As my small contribution, I started compiling a list of these services, which I'm sharing here. I'll keep updating it as I find new ones and receive your suggestions.

If you'd like to contribute to the list, you can write to me at [javierzapata82@gmail.com](mailto:javierzapata82@gmail.com?subject=%5BCoronafreemium%5D%20Service) and if you'd like to help spread the word, use the hashtag [#coronafreemium](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23coronafreemium).

## Learning

- **Domestika (indefinite access):** Selection of creative courses. [Link](https://www.domestika.org/quedateencasa)
- **Capaball (2 months free):** AI-powered training plans. [Link](https://capaball.com/)
- **NeuroK (indefinite access):** Learning community based on neuroeducation. [Link](https://neurok.es/coronavirus-acceso-gratis/)
- **Cambridge University Press (access until end of May):** Cambridge University's academic press. [Link](https://www.cambridge.org/about-us/covid-19/)
- **CodelyTV (1 month free):** Learning platform for developers. [Link](https://twitter.com/CodelyTV/status/1240272977830305792)
- **Coursera (75 free certificates):** Learning platform with courses from universities and companies worldwide. [Link](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/coronavirus-coursera-free-certificate/)
- **Google Cloud (1 month free Coursera/Pluralsight/Qwiklabs):** Cloud computing provider. [Link](https://inthecloud.withgoogle.com/training-discount/register.html)
- **Execute Program (free during April):** Learning platform aimed at web developers. [Link](https://twitter.com/exec_prog/status/1245757921922895872)
- **Pluralsight (free during April):** Learning platform for technology professionals. [Link](https://www.pluralsight.com/blog/news/stay-home-skill-up-for-free)

## Health

- **Fitbit Coach (90 days free):** Video workout routines. [Link](https://blog.fitbit.com/letter-from-ceo/)

## Communication

- **Microsoft Teams (6 months free):** Team communication and collaboration tool. [Link](https://teams.microsoft.com/start)

## Creativity

- **Affinity (90 days free):** Suite of professional creative tools. [Link](https://affinity.serif.com/en-gb/supporting-the-creative-community/)

## Security

- **1Password Business (6 months free):** Cloud-based password manager for businesses. [Link](https://1password.com/business/)

## Administration

- **Infoautónomos (1 month free):** Templates, webinars, guides, and other useful resources for the self-employed. [Link](https://www.infoautonomos.com/login/?next=/cuenta/mes-regalo/)

## Entertainment

- **My Opera Player:** On-demand video opera. [Link](https://likes.movistar.es/cultura-y-ocio/cultura/my-opera-player-gratis-todos)
